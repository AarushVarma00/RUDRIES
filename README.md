# RUDRIES

RUDRIES is a personal project built alongside my journey of learning, creating, and shaping ideas into something of my own.

The project is still in its early stages. Its direction and capabilities will continue to take shape through what I learn, explore, and become capable of building.

## Current Stage

RUDRIES is currently in its foundational stage.

The present focus is on building its core structure and establishing the foundations of the system.

## Future Development

RUDRIES is intended to grow beyond its current form.

As the project develops, its structure and capabilities will expand based on what the system requires and what I become capable of creating.

## Project Philosophy

RUDRIES is not a project I intend to build only after I have learned everything required to create it.

It is something I build as I learn.

Each stage reflects what I understood, what I attempted, what I got wrong, and what I became capable of building next.
